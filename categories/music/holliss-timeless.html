Description

"Timeless" is a beautiful representation of a slow and steady love that never fades. With the rise of Tinder and ghosting, the kind of consistency is harder to find these days. So when you find lifelong partners and forever friends, the memories you make are priceless. Your future looks brighter with them in the picture. Treasure that happy-go-lucky sort of love with a song that’s equally as “timeless”!
