Description

Madhertz comes together with lullabi, Rioux V, Scarlett and Eileen Jaime bring the perfect storm of must hear music on the new song “One of a Kind. When you hear the music, you instantly feel the big energy of the music with every element in the production making you want to dance for every second. To match the sound you get an amazing vocal performance that brings nice appeal, and matched with incredible writing to take the energy to a higher level, to make it that much more exciting to get into.
